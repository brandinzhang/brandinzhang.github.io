## About Me

I am currently a third-year undergraduate student in the College of Information and Electrical Engineering, China Agricultural University. I am fortunate to be advised by [Yanan Wu](https://faculty.cau.edu.cn/wyn1/) and [Yitian Xu](https://faculty.cau.edu.cn/xyt/list.htm).

My research interests lie in optimization theory and the post-training of vision/language pretrained models. I am particularly interested in developing reliable post-training and fine-tuning methods that enable models to adapt rapidly and robustly to downstream tasks and evolving environments.
